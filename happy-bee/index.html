The Happy Bee
Coffee & Pastry Shop

Located in the Short North Arts District of Columbus, Ohio, The Happy Bee has served fresh pastries and coffee for over 15 years. Stop in and try one of our delicious hand-crafted espresso drinks, which always includes at least one seasonal flavor.

At The Happy Bee, we value sustaianability. That's why we offer a 25 cent discount to anyone who brings a reusable cup for to-go orders. Additionally, we donate all of our unsold pastries to local shelters to make sure nothing goes to waste.
